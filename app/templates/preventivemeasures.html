Preventive Measures

We care about your safety and want to help you protect yourself from theft-related crimes. Here are some tips you can follow:

Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Stay vigilant, especially in crowded or unfamiliar areas.

Secure Your Home

Install robust locks on doors and windows, and consider using security cameras or alarm systems for added protection.

Protect Your Valuables

Keep jewelry, electronics, and important documents in a safe or secure, hidden location.

Lock Your Vehicle

Always lock your motorcycle, car doors, and windows, and park in well-lit, populated areas.

Use Anti-Theft Accessories

Opt for anti-theft bags, wallets with RFID protection, or hidden pouches to keep your belongings safe.

Light Up Your Property

Install motion-sensor lights around your home to deter potential intruders from approaching.
